STEM League

For students by students.

Welcome to our website! We are STEM League, an educational organization with the mission to equip student with creative problem solving skills in tech. We offer summer workshops at local libraries to teach young students technical skills including a series of intensive web development and interactive data workshops. We believe that sharing knowledge is the best way to inspire unique innovation in any area of study. We highly encourage you to explore and attend our workshops to learn more about how you can train yourself with the skills to craft your own tech projects!

What We Offer

Coding Workshops

Summer workshops will allow students to learn the basics of coding, from creating your own GitHub to analyzing their down dataset! Make sure to sign up before space runs out!

One-on-One Tutoring

Having a hard time parsing math or science problems? No problem, we area offering one-on-one and group tutoring sessions on various math and science subjects including geometry, algebra, calculus, chemistry, and physics.

Online Courses

Our newest project is to integrate our lessons onto an online platform for accessible learning for those who can't make it to our classes. You can stay updated on our launch here.
